üöÄYour Robotics Journey

From first-time builder to world champion competitor

1

Join Your School Team

Sign up at any of our 15 participating schools. No experience needed - just curiosity and enthusiasm!

2

Learn & Build

Work with coaches and teammates to design, build, and program your robot. Learn block coding or Python.

3

County Competition

Compete against 50+ local teams at the Mercer County Championships. Top teams advance to state.

Represent Mercer County at the WV State Championships in Fairmont. Win awards and qualify for Worlds.

World Championship

Travel to Dallas, Texas to compete against teams from 55+ countries at the VEX Robotics World Championship!
